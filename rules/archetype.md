## Archetypes

Your archetype defines your role when engaged in conflict and determines some of your characters base actions you have access to. Choose one archetype at character creation. When creating new actions you can build on a base action so there will be benefits and also discounts when the actions are aligned with your archetypes role. They can also affect stats.

### Defender

Through armor, agility, or mystical fortitude, Defenders maintain high defense and excel at drawing enemy aggression. They protect vulnerable allies, mitigate incoming damage, and control enemies in close quarters. Defenders can manipulate the position of those engaged in direct melee with them.

**Party Role:** Tank, protector, aggro management, melee control

**Tier 1 Benefit**
Gain +5 Max Vitality.

**Tier 1 Actions**

**Intercept (1 Token) [Active]**
Range: Self
Target: Self & an ally within 3"
Effect: Move up to 3" toward the ally. If ending within 1" of ally, Self & Target: +1 Def vs next Atk.

---

**Stand Ground (1 Token) [Active]**
Range: Self
Target: Self
Effect: Reduce forced movement vs Self by 2" until next turn.

---

**Provoking Strike (1 Token) [Active]**
Range: 0
Target: An enemy
Effect: Atk +1 Res. Max Dmg 2. Hit: +2 Impaired OR +2 Hindered.

---

**Get Behind Me (2 Tokens) [Reaction]**
Range: 3"
Target: An ally
Effect: Swap positions with target. Become target of the Atk. Def + Draw 1 vs this Atk.
Special: Only when an ally within range is targeted by an Atk.

---

## Vanguard

Vanguards unleash devastating damage through aggressive forward momentum and overwhelming force. They charge into the heart of battle, breaking enemy lines and creating opportunities through sheer ferocity. Vanguards excel at burst damage and can use their momentum to reposition enemies they collide with during their assault.

**Party Role:** Burst damage, frontline breaker, aggressive engagement

**Tier 1 Benefit**
Start of turn: -1 Impaired OR -1 Hindered. Gain +1 Max Dmg on all actions until start of next turn.

**Tier 1 Actions**

**Bull Rush (1 Token) [Active]**
Range: Self
Target: Self, then a character at Range 0
Effect: Move up to 3". If ending B2B with a character, Reposition them. If Repositioned, target: 1 dmg.

---

**Devastating Charge (2 Tokens) [Active]**
Range: Self
Target: Self
Effect: Advance up to 5". If Advance > 3", characters B2B: 1 dmg & Push 3".

---

**Leaping Strike (1 Token) [Active]**
Range: 0
Target: An enemy
Effect: Advance up to 3". Atk +1 Res. Max Dmg 2.

---

**Executioner (1 Token) [Active]**
Range: 0
Target: An enemy
Effect: Atk +2 Res. Max Dmg 2.

---

## Skirmisher

Skirmishers eliminate threats through mobility and tactical strikes from advantageous positions. They dart in to attack and withdraw before retaliation, maintaining distance while applying consistent pressure. Skirmishers excel at applying damage over time effects and can reposition themselves or enemies to maintain optimal engagement range.

**Party Role:** Mobile damage dealer, tactical positioning, sustained pressure

**Tier 1 Benefit**
Start of turn: Move 2" OR -All Hindered. This Move does not provoke Reactions.

**Tier 1 Actions**

**Hit and Run (2 Tokens) [Active]**
Range: 0
Target: An enemy
Effect: Atk +2 Res. Max Dmg 3. After the Atk, Place up to 3" away.

---

**Viper Strike (1 Token) [Active]**
Range: 0
Target: An enemy
Effect: Atk +1 Res. Max Dmg 2. Hit: +2 Afflicted.

---

**Dodge (1 Token) [Reaction]**
Range: Self
Target: Self
Effect: Retreat up to 3".
Special: When targeted by an Atk. If ending out of Range, Atk Misses.

---

**Backstab (1 Token) [Active]**
Range: 0
Target: An enemy
Effect: Atk +2 Res. Max Dmg 3. +1 Exposed.
Special: Only if target is Exposed.

---

## Tactician

Masters of battlefield manipulation, Tacticians apply conditions and control enemy positioning. They excel at debuffing foes, creating advantageous situations, and limiting enemy options through strategic use of conditions and forced movement.

**Party Role:** Condition application, positioning control, battlefield shaping

**Tier 1 Benefit**
Once per round: You or ally within Range 6" may perform a Reaction for 0 Tokens. (Essence costs still apply).

**Tier 1 Actions**

**Tactical Reposition (2 Tokens) [Active]**
Range: 6"
Target: Up to 2 allies
Effect: Move each target up to 3". Closest enemy to each target: +1 Impaired.

---

**Suppression (1 Token) [Active]**
Range: 0
Target: Point B2B
Effect: Place 3" Circle. Start Move in area: +2 Hindered. Enter/End in area: +2 Exposed. Sustain.

---

**Disrupt (1 Token) [Reaction]**
Range: 6"
Target: An enemy
Effect: Negate Help. +2 Impaired.
Special: When target uses Help.

---

## Anchor

The backbone of any party, Anchors keep their allies fighting effectively. They provide healing, remove harmful conditions, manage party aggro distribution, coordinate energy resources, and apply buffs to enhance their teammates' capabilities.

**Party Role:** Healer, cleanser, buffer, resource management, team coordination

**Tier 1 Benefit**
Gain +3 Max Essence. Start of turn: Choose Allies within Range 6" gain 1 Essence OR Allies within Range 6" gain 1 Vitality.

**Tier 1 Actions**

**Rally (2 Tokens) [Active]**
Range: 6"
Target: All allies within range
Effect: Each ally: -2 condition stacks, gain 1 Vitality & 1 Essence.

---

**Mend (1 Token) [Active]**
Range: 0
Target: An ally
Effect: Target gains 2 Vitality.

---

**Transference (1 Token) [Active]**
Range: 0
Target: An ally
Effect: Spend up to 4 Essence. Target gains equal Essence.

---

**Aid (1 Token) [Active]**
Range: 0
Target: An ally
Effect: Remove all stacks of 1 Condition type.

---

## Fixer

Versatile and adaptable, the Fixers have access to a wider range of general actions than other archetypes. They move quickly and can fill multiple roles as needed, making them invaluable for handling unexpected situations.

**Party Role:** Flexible utility, backup for any role, speed and versatility

**Tier 1 Benefit**
Gain +1 Action Slot. Start of turn: +1 Def OR +1 Max Dmg OR +1" to all Moves. Cannot choose same benefit two turns in a row. Lasts until start of next turn.

**Tier 1 Actions**

**Adaptable Strike (1 Token) [Active]**
Range: 0
Target: An enemy
Effect: Atk +1 Res. Max Dmg 2. Hit: Push 1" OR +1 of any Condition.

---

**Quick Fix (1 Token) [Active]**
Range: 0
Target: An ally
Effect: -1 Condition OR Gain 1 Vitality.

---

**Dirty Trick (1 Token) [Reaction]**
Range: 0
Target: An enemy
Effect: Reposition Self 2". Target: 1 dmg & +1 Impaired. Gain +1 Def vs the Atk.
Special: When targeted by an Atk.
