# Equipment

## Currency & Economy

The economy is based on **Spirit Chips**â€”crystal shards containing standardized amounts of unattuned essence. While higher-tier chips are used for cultivation, lower-tier chips serve as currency.

### Currency Tiers

| Tier  | Chip Name         | Color / Appearance       | Market Use                                 | Exchange Rate |
|:------|:------------------|:-------------------------|:-------------------------------------------|:--------------|
| **1** | **Iron Chip**     | Dull Grey / Slate        | Daily food, mundane gear, simple weapons.  | Base Unit     |
| **2** | **Copper Chip**   | Orange-Brown / Amber     | Basic pills, Tier 1 cultivation resources. | 100 Iron      |
| **3** | **Silver Chip**   | Bright White / Chrome    | Fragments, Tier 2 materials, fine weapons. | 100 Copper    |
| **4** | **Gold Chip**     | Brilliant Yellow / Aurum | Tier 3-4 treasures, rare artifacts.        | 100 Silver    |
| **5** | **Platinum Chip** | Iridescent Violet        | Elite commerce, Sect Leader dealings.      | 100 Gold      |
| **6** | **Star-Metal**    | Pulsing Black & White    | Planetary-scale trade.                     | 100 Platinum  |

### Starting Wealth

Your starting wealth depends on your **Identity** and background. Choose the category that best fits your character concept (subject to GM approval).

| Status        | Starting Funds | Description                                                                              |
|:--------------|:---------------|:-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Destitute** | **20 Iron**    | Beggar, slave, or ascetic. You have the clothes on your back and little else.            |
| **Common**    | **150 Iron**   | Farmer, soldier, or laborer. You can afford basic gear and a weapon.                     |
| **Well-Off**  | **400 Iron**   | Merchant, minor noble, or elite guard. You can afford good armor and weapons.            |
| **Wealthy**   | **10 Copper**  | Major noble, sect heir, or wealthy merchant. Money is rarely an issue for mundane needs. |
